Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoBuild AI collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Platform or interact with our website. We take your privacy seriously and keep data collection to what is genuinely necessary.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information — name and email address when you join the waitlist or create an account
  • Usage data — how you interact with the Platform, including features used, session duration, and errors encountered
  • Uploaded content — 3D model files, G-code, and print parameters you submit to the RL Slicer or Live Monitor
  • Technical data — browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers collected automatically when you access the Platform
  • Communications — messages you send us via the contact form or email

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Platform
  • Process your 3D models and generate optimised print strategies
  • Send you early access updates and onboarding communications
  • Respond to support enquiries and contact form messages
  • Monitor Platform performance and diagnose technical issues
  • Improve our AI models using aggregated and anonymised print data

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

3. Uploaded Models and Print Data

Files you upload (STL, OBJ, G-code) are processed to provide the services you request and are not shared with third parties. We may use anonymised, aggregated print metrics (e.g., layer counts, travel distances, material types) to train and improve our reinforcement learning models. We will not use identifiable files from your account for this purpose without your consent.

You may request deletion of your uploaded files at any time by contacting us at hello@autobuild3dcp.com.

4. Data Sharing

We share personal information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — infrastructure and hosting providers who process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — where we are required by law, regulation, or court order to disclose information
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice provided to affected users

5. Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Uploaded files are retained for the duration of your access period. You may request deletion at any time.

Usage logs and anonymised analytics may be retained for longer periods to support Platform improvement.

6. Security

We apply industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@autobuild3dcp.com.

If you are in the European Economic Area or UK, you have rights under GDPR. If you are in Australia, the Australian Privacy Act 1988 applies.

8. Cookies

The Platform may use essential cookies to maintain sessions and remember preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or behavioural tracking cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify early access users of material changes via email. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@autobuild3dcp.com or via the contact page.
