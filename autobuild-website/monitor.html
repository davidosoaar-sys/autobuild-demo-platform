Live Monitor

The extra set of eyes
that never blinks.

Claude Vision analyses every frame of your concrete print in real time. Bead deviation. Surface quality. Layer integrity. All logged automatically.

What it monitors.

01 — Detection

Bead deviation

Angle measurement on every bead, every layer. Deviations classified by severity and logged with frame-accurate timestamps.

02 — Quality

Surface quality

Consistency and uniformity checks across each pass. Irregular surfaces flagged before they compound into structural issues.

03 — Structure

Layer integrity

Bonding and continuity analysis between layers. Gaps, voids, and adhesion anomalies detected and recorded in the event log.

How it works.

01
Connect your camera

Any standard camera works. Point it at your print head and connect through the Monitor interface — no special hardware required.

02
AI analyses every frame

Claude Vision processes your video feed continuously, running deviation detection and surface analysis on each frame as it arrives.

03
Alerts fire instantly

Critical deviations trigger immediate alerts. You know about a problem the moment it happens — not when you review footage later.

04
Everything logged automatically

Every event is timestamped and saved. When the print ends, a complete quality report is generated — no manual documentation needed.
